# Stakeholders

The purpose of this stakeholders exercise is to focus our attention on the humans involved in the system. We want to think about the different types of users that the system will have, but we also want to consider staffing, who our partners might be, and what type of vendor relationships we might have. Sometimes people will describe their role as being one of many hats — let's capture those hats.

This workshop is for humans. Please do not use AI to help make observations.

## Observe

Document as many stakeholders as you can think of. List one person (or persona) per reply and include as much information as you have.

It could be as simple as

```
Account Manager
```

or as abstract as

```
Corporate Social Creator
```

or as complete as

```
James Johnson, CEO, james@big-co.com, 888-555-1234
```

When you're ready to start, reply with a thumbs up. When you have emptied your mind of stakeholders, react with a check-mark.
