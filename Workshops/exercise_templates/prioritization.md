### Observe

When making your observations, provide two simple comma-delimited lists. First order the ideas from lowest to highest **impact**, and then from least to most **feasible**. For example:

```
impact: 3,2,1,9,4,8,10,5,6,7
feasibility: 3,1,2,5,6,8,4,7,9,10
```

Tip: copy the idea list into a markdown file, use opt-arrow (or alt-arrow) hot keys to move lines up and down to get them in order, then list the numbers here in that order.

When you're ready to start, reply with a thumbs up. When you have emptied your mind of all observations, react with a check-mark.

### Reflect

Review the ranking observations, then compile average rankings for each number, and group ideas under four categories:

- High impact, high feasibility ideas are considered **no-brainers**
- High impact, low feasibility ideas are considered **big bets**
- Low impact, high feasibility ideas are considered **utilities**
- Low impact, low feasibility ideas are **unwise**
