The purpose of this priority exercise is to help identify the no-brainer ideas from a backlog of great ideas. We will ask each of you to rank the following ideas in two ways: **impact** and **feasibility**.

## Observe

When making your observations, provide two simple comma-delimited lists. First order the ideas from lowest to highest **impact** (least to most helpful), and then from least to most **feasible** (hardest to easiest). For example:

```
impact: 3,2,1,9,4,8,10,5,6,7
feasibility: 3,1,2,5,6,8,4,7,9,10
```

Tip: copy the idea list into a markdown file, use opt-arrow (or alt-arrow) hot keys to move lines up and down to get them in order, then list the numbers here in that order.

When you're ready to start, reply with a thumbs up. When you have emptied your mind of all observations, react with a check-mark.
