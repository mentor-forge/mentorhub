# Stakeholders

The purpose of this stakeholders exercise is to focus our attention on the different types of people that might be considered a _`Persona Name`_ in the system. Sometimes people will describe their role as being one of many hats — let's capture those hats as well.

This workshop is for humans. Please do not use AI to help make observations.

### Observe

Document as many different _`Persona Name`_'s as you can think of. List one person per reply and include as much information as you have.

It could be as simple as

```
Account Manager
```

or as abstract as

```
Corporate Social Creator
```

or as complete as

```
James Johnson, CEO, james@big-co.com, 888-555-1234
```

When you're ready to start, reply with a thumbs up. When you have emptied your mind of stakeholders, react with a check-mark.
